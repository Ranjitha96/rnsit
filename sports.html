SPORTS AND CULTURE

Sports Activites

The Department of Physical education is headed by Mr.K.Purnachandra having 9 years experience. The sports facility is one of the best of its kind in the state having good infrastructure for various games and other sport activities. Various facilities available in the campus are

  • Basket Ball Court
  • Football Ground
  • Volley Ball Court
  • Cricket Ground
  • Throw Ball Court

Indoor Games

A State of the Art, international level cricket ground with an area of 17,000 sq. mtrs. is under construction. The College is organizing a Basket Ball Rolling Trophy tournament annually. Regular training Programs for games like Basket ball, Cricket, Football etc., are conducted for the students.

The Institute is planning to have an Indoor Sports Complex, consisting of two badminton courts, Basket ball courts in addition to Table Tennis, Carom and Chess.

  • Table Tennis
  • Chess
  • Carrom

Fitness Department
